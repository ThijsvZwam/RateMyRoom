RateMyRoom

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Who We Are

RateMyRoom ("we", "us", "our") is a website located at morsenity.com that allows users to upload photos of their rooms and receive AI-generated ratings and feedback.

2. What Data We Collect

When you use RateMyRoom, we may collect the following information:

  • Room photos — images you voluntarily upload for rating
  • Nicknames — the display name you choose (or an auto-generated one)
  • IP address — collected automatically to prevent abuse and spam
  • Usage data — standard server logs including browser type and timestamps

We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information. No registration is required to use the site.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interest — to operate the website, prevent abuse, and maintain security
  • Consent — when you voluntarily upload content and submit it to the public leaderboard

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To display your room rating on the public leaderboard
  • To prevent abuse, spam, and manipulation of scores
  • To moderate reported content and maintain a safe community
  • To improve and operate the website

5. Public Nature of Submissions

Images and nicknames you upload are publicly visible on the leaderboard and other parts of the website. By submitting, you acknowledge that your room photo and chosen nickname will be visible to all visitors. Do not upload images containing personal information you do not wish to share publicly.

6. AI Processing of Images

Uploaded images are sent to OpenRouter for processing by AI models to generate ratings and feedback. Images may be processed on servers located outside your country depending on the infrastructure used by our hosting and AI providers. By uploading an image you consent to this processing. Please review OpenRouter's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

7. IP Address Storage

IP addresses are stored temporarily for abuse prevention and security purposes. They are not shared publicly and are automatically purged after a limited period unless required for ongoing moderation or security investigations.

8. Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to show personalized ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings. For more information visit Google's Privacy & Terms.

9. Cookies

We currently do not use first-party cookies. Google AdSense and other third-party services may set their own cookies on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

10. Data Location

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country depending on the infrastructure used by our hosting provider (Render) and AI providers (OpenRouter). These providers maintain their own data protection practices in accordance with applicable law.

11. Data Retention

Leaderboard entries including nicknames and room photos are stored indefinitely unless removed by moderation or upon request. IP addresses are stored temporarily for abuse prevention. You can request removal of your submission by contacting us.

12. Children's Privacy

RateMyRoom is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that personal data from a child under 16 has been submitted, we will remove it promptly. If you believe a child has submitted data, please contact us.

13. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in the Netherlands: Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

14. Third-Party Services

  • OpenRouter — AI model routing for room ratings
  • Google AdSense — advertising
  • Render — server hosting

15. Contact

For questions, data deletion requests, or privacy concerns, contact us at: contact@morsenity.com

16. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
